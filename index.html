Chattanooga Hackers Anonymous (CHA) is a local collective of ethical hackers, cybersecurity professionals, and infosec enthusiasts in Tennessee. We focus on education, community building, and responsible security research.

Our group hosts regular meetups, training sessions, and participates in information security events. Whether you're interested in penetration testing, red team operations, blue team defense, or just learning about cybersecurity, you'll find like-minded individuals in our community.
